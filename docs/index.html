Connections

What are all these things? ====>>>>

Connect the CANH, CANL pins of your CANWork to your target CAN bus. If your USB power supply ground is common with other devices on the bus, you do not need to connect ground on the terminal side for the CAN bus to function properly.

Do not connect the 5v output of the CANWork unless you need to power a target with 5v: this is an output only!
